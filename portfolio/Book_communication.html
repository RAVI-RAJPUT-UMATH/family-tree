COMMUNIICATION & BOOK CLUB


COURSE OVERVIEW
--Book club and Social Emotional

1. To inculcate a habit of book reading among the students, for improving their vocabulary, comprehension, critical thinking and reflecting on the perspectives provided by various authors on varying titles.

2. To make the students self-aware and manage their emotions, develop social awareness and social skills. The course should help the student’s build and maintain healthy relationships through establishing a safe, positive, and mutually beneficial environment, both at the workplace and in their personal lives.


--COMMUNICATION

The objective of the course is to develop students’ competency in English language communication with respect to listening, speaking, reading and writing. After the course, the students should be able to:
1. Grasp and implement the foundational principles of communication within their interactions.
2. Think freely, recall the topics taught in class and implement them.
3. Engage in conversations using grammatically accurate English, while also incorporating nuanced phonetics, vocabulary, intonation, and improved pronunciation which is free of MTI.
4. Public speaking skills.
